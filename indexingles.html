Mission and Vision

Mission

Our mission is to help individuals discover their true potential and create an authentic and coherent personal image that reflects their identity and values. We are committed to providing high-quality tools and resources to support personal growth and image transformation, aiming to enhance confidence, self-esteem, and the lifestyle of our audience.

Vision

Our vision is to help individuals reach their maximum potential by creating an authentic and coherent personal image that reflects their identity and values. We want to inspire confidence, self-esteem, and a balanced lifestyle by providing tools and resources for our clients to achieve their goals and live a fuller and more satisfying life. Our goal is to empower individuals to be the best versions of themselves, helping them discover their hidden skills and talents, and develop a personal image that allows them to feel secure, confident, and authentic.

Objectives

General Objective

Implement a personalized and accessible image consulting system that supports individuals in the proper selection of their clothing style, personal care products, and presentation strategies, contributing to better self-perception, greater emotional well-being, and enhanced social and professional opportunities.

Specific Objectives

  • Develop a set of personalized guides and recommendations based on the results of well-known and current studies, which help individuals select their clothing and style according to their body type, personal preferences, and social or professional contexts.
  • Execute a promotional campaign on social media, local media, and nearby communities to raise awareness of the benefits of our image consulting system and attract a diverse audience.
  • Include interactive features such as virtual fitting rooms, style simulators, and video tutorials on the website, thus facilitating an engaging and educational user experience.

Legal Framework

Licenses and Certifications

Image consulting professionals, such as image consultants and stylists, must meet specific licensing and certification requirements to practice legally. For example, in some U.S. states, cosmetology licenses are required to provide advice on personal care and makeup products.

Protection of Personal Data

There are specific laws that protect the privacy and personal data of clients. For example, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union sets requirements for the collection, storage, and use of personal data. It is essential to obtain informed consent from clients and ensure that their data is protected.

Advertising and Marketing

The advertising and marketing laws for our project ensure that the information provided about products and services is truthful and not misleading. Advertising must comply with specific regulations to avoid unverified exaggerated claims about the results of the products or services offered.
